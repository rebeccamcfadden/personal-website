Minecraft Gameplay Systems SWE Intern, Microsoft/Mojang

During the summer of 2020, I worked as a SWE Intern with the Minecraft Bedrock Edition Gameplay Systems team. Minecraft is a game that is largely defined by its "modding", or content-modification, community. The Java edition of the game, which is available for PC, is easily modded. Since the Bedrock edition is written in C++, and is playable on consoles, mobile, and PCs, modifying the executable code is not an option. The Bedrock team is attempting to provide the modding experience through data-driven "resource" and "behavior packs". I worked on event systems for items in the behavior packs to allow users to create custom items that can interact with other in-game entities.
