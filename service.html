Scratch CS Education

I volunteered with a local elementary school in high school to teach computer science to third and fourth graders for 30 mins once a week. As part of my senior capstone project, I created a 'video game bootcamp' curriculum and taught that three times a week. It was really cool to see the kids get excited about computer science. I was also able to pass my curriculum along to their teachers, and one of them still teaches coding today.

Intern Give Day

As part of Intern Day, I had the opportunity to participate in the Giving Program at Microsoft. I chose to volunteer to paint benches as part of an initiative known as Buddy Bench. The goal of the bench is to help kids who are shy or lonely reach out and find friends to play with.

AWiCS Mentorship

I started a mentorship program for Aggie Women in Computer Science. My goal was to pair underclassmen women with upperclassmen who have experience in not only CS classes, but the culture and environment of the workplace. The program kicked off this year with 25 mentorship pairs. I hope that this program can grow over time and encompass freshman women who have not yet decided whether they want to go into computer science to give them a sense of community.
